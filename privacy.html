If we obtain actual knowledge that we have collected Personal Data from a person under the age of 13 or a person between 13 and 17 years of age who does not have a parent’s or a guardian’s consent to use Invibe Services, we will take steps to remove that person’s Personal Data permanently from our databases.

6. Collection of Personal Data

Personal Data That You Provide

It is our ambition to make your use of the Invibe Platform and Invibe Services a good experience. Invibe collects information that you voluntarily enter, including Personal Data, within the Invibe Platform. Invibe collects Personal Data about you and your friends who use Invibe Services, from you and from the Social Network connected to the Invibe Platform (including your profile photo), in order to provide you with a more personalized experience. We may collect information from your Social Network profile that you have permitted to be displayed through the Invibe Platform and we may also collect information that you submit when using Invibe Services. For example, when you send a Gift to your friends, we collect and associate certain of your non-personally identifiable demographic information (like age, gender, your concrete location and the receptor concrete location) with the specific merchant (“Merchant”) for the Gift in order to create a better and smarter gifting experience. We may also request optional information from you to support and enhance your use of Invibe Services.
  • Identification data (e.g. name, email address);
  • Profile information (e.g. age, gender, nationality);
  • Location information (e.g. concrete friends or people nearby location)
  • Electronic identification data (e.g. IP address, cookies);
  • Content that you submit during your use of Invibe Services, such as photos, reviews, greetings and other information you choose to submit.
When you purchase a Paid Gifts, you will need to submit credit card or other payment information (Paypal) so that our service providers can process your payment for the gift card. All payments are processed with due observance of any applicable security obligations. When you sign up for or win any contests, sweepstakes or other activities that we make available on the Invibe Platform, we will also collect the information designated along with such activity, which may include your contact information such as your address and phone number in order to provide you with Invibe Services.

Location information

Our Service is also about sharing location and finding friends and others nearby, so we will use your location information to provide the basic functionality of the Service, such as displaying your location on the Services map to other users when you check-in using Third Party Services (social networks) or your specifically “on” in the application. Users viewing this information may use it or disclose it to other individuals or entities outside of our control and without your knowledge. Your location information may be subject to abuse, misuse, and monitoring by others, and you share your location at your own risk.

Personal Data from Other Sources

We may receive Personal Data about you from other sources such as the Social Network connected to the Invibe Platform, as well as Merchants and other third parties that help us improve your experience of Invibe Services and generally make Invibe Services better. Invibe may associate this information with the other Personal Data that Invibe has collected about you.

Email and Email Addresses

If you send an email to us or fill out our “Feedback” form through the Invibe Platform, we will collect your email address and the full content of your email, including attached files and other information you provide in order to document and to further develop Invibe Services based on user feedback. If you provide feedback to us, we may use and disclose such feedback to our Merchants to improve or modify Invibe Services.

You may at any time opt-out from any further advertisements to your email address by sending a notice to Invibe at support@invibe.me.

Information Collected via Technology

As you use Invibe Services, certain information may also be passively collected and stored on our or our service providers’ server logs, including your IP address, browser type and operating system. We may also use cookies (as defined below) and navigational data like Uniform Resource Locators (”URLs”) to gather information regarding the date and time of your visit and the solutions and information for which you searched and viewed, or on which of the advertisements displayed on the Invibe Platform you clicked. This type of information is collected to make Invibe Services more useful to you and to tailor the experience with Invibe Services to meet your special interests and needs.
For example, we, or our service providers, may track your IP Address when you access the Invibe Platform to assist with ad targeting. An “Internet protocol address” or “IP Address” is a number that is automatically assigned to your Device when you use the Internet. In some cases your IP Address stays the same from browser session to browser session, but if you use a consumer Internet access provider, your IP Address probably varies from session to session.

Cookies

“Cookies” are small pieces of information that Invibe sends to and stores on the Device that you use when accessing the Invibe Platform.
Invibe uses cookies to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience with the Invibe Platform and to improve Invibe Services. Cookies help us evaluate what types of information that might be of interest to you and other users, to improve our and our Merchants’ advertisements and to protect you and Invibe from unlawful activities. We also use cookies to know when you are interacting with Merchants or other third parties.
In order to use Invibe Services, your Device must accept cookies. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them). Persistent cookies can be removed or blocked by following your Internet browser´s help file directions. But some aspects of the Invibe Platform may not work properly and you may not be able to use Invibe Services if our cookies are removed or blocked.
